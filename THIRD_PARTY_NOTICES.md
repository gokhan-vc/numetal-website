# Third-party notices

Numetal's `/fees` page renders the fee engine as a *machine report* — a dense,
boxed, monospace system-readout (dot-leader `KEY ···· VALUE` rows, bar gauges, a
teletext/CRT treatment). That report **format** is inspired by the open-source
**usgraphics/usgc-machine-report** (TR-100), which is distributed under the
BSD 3-Clause License. We re-implemented the layout independently in our own
markup and styles; this notice is provided as attribution and good-faith
compliance.

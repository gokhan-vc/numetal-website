Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Numetal (“Numetal”, “we”, “us”) handles information in connection with the website at numetal.xyz and the Numetal Discord application (the “bot”), together the “Services”. We collect as little as possible.

01Information we collect

Website. We do not ask for or collect personal information through numetal.xyz. The site serves static content and reads public on-chain data from the Base network. Our infrastructure provider (Cloudflare) processes standard technical request data (such as IP address and browser metadata) to deliver and secure the site.

Discord bot. When a server moderator runs one of the bot’s commands, the bot processes the Discord identifiers necessary to carry out that action — the target user, the acting moderator, the server, and the relevant message — solely to perform the requested moderation (timeout, ban, message removal) and to write an entry to that server’s moderation-log channel. The bot does not read or store general message content, does not build user profiles, and does not retain personal data beyond what is needed to perform the requested action.

02How we use information

We use the limited information above only to operate, maintain, and secure the Services. Our lawful basis is our legitimate interest in operating, securing, and delivering the Services (GDPR Art 6(1)(f)). We do not sell or share personal information, and we do not use it for advertising, targeted advertising, or profiling (including under the CCPA/CPRA).

03Service providers

We rely on third-party infrastructure to run the Services: Cloudflare (hosting, security, delivery), Discord (the platform the bot operates on, governed by Discord’s own policies), and public blockchain RPC providers used to read on-chain data. On-chain data on Base is inherently public.

04Cookies & tracking

numetal.xyz does not use advertising or analytics tracking cookies. Cloudflare may set strictly necessary cookies for security and to operate the site.

05Data retention

We retain technical/security logs only as long as needed for security and operation. Moderation-log entries created by the bot live in your own Discord server and are controlled by you, the server owner.

06Your choices & rights

Under the GDPR and UK GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and port your personal data, to object to processing, and to lodge a complaint with your data-protection supervisory authority (GDPR Art 77). To exercise any of these, contact contact@numetal.xyz. Server owners control the bot’s configuration and the moderation-log channel within their own servers.

07Children’s privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 16 (or the applicable digital-consent age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

08Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “last updated” date above reflects the latest version. Continued use of the Services after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

09Contact

Questions about this policy: reach us in the official Numetal Discord, or at contact@numetal.xyz.
